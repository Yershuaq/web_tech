The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People

Author: Stephen R. Covey

Published: August 15, 1989

Price: $15.99

This book offers timeless principles on personal effectiveness and leadership. It teaches readers how to align their principles with universal truths and achieve long-lasting success.

Grit: The Power of Passion and Perseverance

Author: Angela Duckworth

Published: May 3, 2016

Price: $14.99

Angela Duckworth explains how grit—a combination of passion and perseverance—is a key factor in long-term success. This book will inspire you to keep pushing forward no matter the obstacles.

Emotional Intelligence

Author: Daniel Goleman

Published: September 27, 1995

Price: $13.99

Daniel Goleman's groundbreaking book explains the importance of emotional intelligence in relationships, work, and personal life. Learn how to develop and leverage your emotional intelligence for success.
